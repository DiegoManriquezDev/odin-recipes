Burger and Fries

This is a delicious recipe for a bacon burger

Ingredient:

  • Bread
  • Hamburguer meat
  • Bacon
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Sauces

How to prepare meat:

  1. In a bowl we place the meat, which will season with salt, garlic powder and pepper to taste, leave in the refrigerator about 25 minutes before using.
  2. Last 25 minutes we will make balls with the meat carefully and we fry crushing or will roast over low heat, until they are desired doneness.

How to prepare burger:

  1. Then we will warm the bread in the pan for a more delicious flavor, followed to this will place the meat.
  2. Now we add a special sauce consisting of mayonnaise and mustard mix evenly, stir to taste and place it above our flesh.
  3. The following will be put Gouda or cheddar cheese to taste, also add one or two leaves of fresh lettuce (previously washed) and a slice of tomato, a slice of onion and pickles.
  4. Finally add the top of the bread and we have our burger finished.

Now comes the most important and fun part, eat! Repeat the process again and again why make one, when you can make two or three?
